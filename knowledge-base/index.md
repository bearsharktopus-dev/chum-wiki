# Chum Knowledge Base

Welcome to the wiki for **Chum**, a web serial set in an alternate-history Philadelphia where superpowers emerged in 1981 and reshaped every aspect of human civilization.

The story follows **Samantha "Sam" Small**, a 15-year-old Jewish girl from Tacony who becomes the vigilante **Bloodhound** - and the investigative team she builds to take on a conspiracy that reaches from the streets of Northeast Philly to the halls of Congress.
