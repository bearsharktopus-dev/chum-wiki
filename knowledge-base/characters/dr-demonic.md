## Background

Dr. Demonic is the medic at Douglas Furr's underground metahuman fight club, working out of a bedsheet-curtained corner of the basement (signed, in Comic Sans, "DR. DEMONIC'S OFFICE - HOURS: WHENEVER SOMEONE GETS HURT - NO INSURANCE ACCEPTED - TIPS APPRECIATED"). He has been "touching people's injuries for nine years."

He tried to work in hospitals and could not. His power does not *manage* pain the way clinical medicine prioritizes - it *compresses* the healing, and with it all of the associated pain, into a brief, maximal burst. The ethics boards balked at informed consent for "a procedure that is guaranteed to be the most painful experience of a patient's life." So he works the basement, where patients understand exactly what his help costs and choose it anyway - "consent in a way that a hospital form will never be." He takes a cut, helps the fighters, and keeps Douglas Furr's roster in working condition.

### Abilities

- **Healing-time compression** - By touch, Dr. Demonic accelerates a body's own natural healing, collapsing a recovery that would take hours or days into seconds. The catch is that the patient experiences *all* of the discomfort of that healing in those same seconds - a tower of compressed pain that Sam (Superbeast), no stranger to injury, called "the worst thing I've ever experienced." A normal nose break takes him about four minutes of dosed sessions; a bad compound fracture, around thirty minutes. He works in measured doses and can do partial treatments that reach "functional" without the full timeline compression.
- **Diagnostic touch** - Can feel the difference between healing factors by hand; correctly identified that Sam's regeneration was "significantly stronger than one-point-five-ex" despite her cover story, declining to press her on it.

### Limitations

- His power accelerates healing but does nothing for comfort; it is unusable in any setting requiring conventional informed consent, which is why he works outside the licensed medical system.

### Training & Skills

- Nine years of practical field-medicine experience working on metahuman combat injuries; calloused, scarred hands from a lifetime of touching people in pain.
- A thorough, accurate clinical vocabulary (he correctly names a "bilateral periorbital hematoma" before translating it to "two black eyes").

## Personality

Behind an absurd, fully-committed Frankenstein's-monster costume - green-gray makeup, magnetic neck bolts, a flat-top wig, a too-short lab coat, platform boots that push him past six and a half feet - Dr. Demonic has the warm, tired, professional manner of a good EMT. He is honest to a fault about how much his treatment will hurt ("it's gonna suck balls"), and he delivers hard truths without drama. His signature warning to durable fighters is the thematic heart of his role: durability is "a gift and a trap... you stop noticing when you're hurt because you know you'll heal. And then one day you get hurt in a way you don't heal from and you don't notice until it's too late." He frames himself plainly: "I'm not your dad. I get a cut off the top, I get to help people, boss man keeps his fighters in working condition. Everyone wins."
